OUR STRY

How we decided that we must
be together forever!

Usually, when marriage thought comes to our mind we always think about finding the right person.
What difference does it make whether you find them on your own or are introduced by your parents?
Nothing..., it's just a change in name (love marriage or arranged marriage).
It doesn't matter how much love we have in the beginning, but how much love we build till the end of our life.
And also it is not always about finding the right person but being the right person and creating the right relationship with our life partner.
So we both were open to the idea of arranged marriage and left that resposnsibility to our parents.


The best kind of relationships begin unexpectedly.
Yes, its true...
We too didn't had believe in this statement till we met each other few days back.
Our families were introduced to each other by our well wishers.


After having some chit chat with each other, unknowingly we both felt the same thing that we are XEROX copies of each other.
Our mind and heart functions in similar way. We guess that the god might have made us like this to be a perfect match for each other.
What else we need other than this?
We flagged green signal to our parents to proceed....


Our new year was started very newly with our special and lovable talks

Though our relationship was primarily long distance, our commitment & love made things work and outweighed all the roadblocks.
and after a point, we decided not to look back...
